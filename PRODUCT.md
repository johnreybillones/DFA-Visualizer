# Automata Visualizer

## Register

product

## Users

Students, instructors, and reviewers who need to inspect deterministic finite automata, test candidate strings, and understand each transition without relying on backend services or static screenshots.

## Product Purpose

Provide a frontend-only DFA workspace that makes two supplied automata legible, interactive, and explainable. The product should help users verify acceptance or rejection, inspect the exact transition trace, and understand graph structure through precise animation and state highlighting.

## Brand Tone

Precise, technical, instructional, and restrained. The interface should feel like an analytical instrument, not a marketing dashboard.

## Strategic Principles

- Show the automaton as data, not as a flattened image.
- Preserve graph integrity on all screen sizes through pan and zoom instead of destructive reflow.
- Keep validation local, deterministic, and explainable.
- Use visual emphasis only when it communicates simulation state.

## Anti-References

- Rounded SaaS analytics dashboards
- Glassmorphism overlays and soft shadow stacks
- Playful illustration-heavy learning apps
- Purple-gradient startup landing page tropes
